Title: Michael H. Borkowski - Assistant Professor of Computer Science

---

Michael Borkowski earned his PhD from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering
at the University of California, San Diego.

His research interests are in software verification, type theory, and interactive theorem
provers. Dr. Borkowski is interested in developing software verification techniques to make
correct and performant software systems easier to write and understand, as well as having
stronger theoretical soundness guarantees.

Contact: mhborkow@purdue.edu

Courses taught include:
CS 18000 - Problem Solving and Object-Oriented Programming (CS 180)
CS 25100 - Data Structures and Algorithms (CS 251)
