Title: Tony Bergstrom - Associate Teaching Professor of Computer Science

---

Tony Bergstrom is an Associate Teaching Professor of Computer Science at Purdue University.
He joined the department in Spring 2019.

Education:
PhD, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Computer Science (2011)
MS, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Computer Science (2006)
BS, Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, Computer Science and Mathematics (2004)

Contact: bgstm@purdue.edu
Office: DSAI 1119F (West Lafayette), SLPI 280F (Indianapolis)

Courses taught include:
CS 18000 - Problem Solving and Object-Oriented Programming (CS 180)
CS 24200 - Introduction to Data Science
CS 19000 - Data Engineering in Python
